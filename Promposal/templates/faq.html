Promposals FAQ

What is this website?

This website is a way for seniors who are wishing to plan a promposal. It's to ensure that no two people have a promposal at the same time. This website keeps track of all the school days for the rest of the year until Prom and records which days are taken.

How do you use it?

In the top, you can see there is a navigation bar. Clicking on "Promposals Calendar" will bring you to the main page. Here, you can input your Stuyvesant email, OSIS, and the date you wish to prompose to your significant other. We will be checking the database every so often and if you input these fields incorrectly or input nonsense, you will be unsigned up for the date.
Also, the website only shows all available dates. If someone signs up for a date, the date will disappear from all the options you can choose from.

What if I forgot what date I signed up for?

Click on "Forgot your Date?" on the navigation bar on top. Input your OSIS and voil√†! Your date will show up.

How come some dates are missing?

There can be two reason:

  • The date is already chosen by someone else.
  • The date is on a weekend, a day where there's no school, or a parent teacher conference.

If all chosen dates are removed from the website, how come I got an error saying someone has chosen a date I chose?

The reason is because both you and that someone went on the website at around the same time, so the date is shown as available on both users. The first person chooses the date first, removing it as an option, but that doesn't get updates and your end because you haven't refreshed your page yet. So to fix this, just refresh.
